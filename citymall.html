Kota, the prodigal growing town is situated in south east Rajasthan’s Hadoti region, on the east bank of the Chambal. Kota has a glorious history of atomic power plants, Kota stone and heavy industries. As far as art and textile is concerned, Kota-Bundi school of miniatures stand a class apart and the legendary Kota-doria saris are worn by women all over the nation. Today, the city is best known for its exceptional coaching institutes adding new dimensions to the preparation of Engineering and Medical Entrance examinations. The results have been phenomenal to the extent that over 70000 aspiring students from all over the country are heading towards this city to frame their future. Thus, while Kota is developing future of millions, City Mall aims at developing the future of this growing town. The City Mall will be coming up on the city’s most important thoroughfare, adjacent to the Instrumentation Township and only a kilometer from Talwandi, the main institutional hub. A 15 minutes drive will take you to the railway station and the bus stand while the airport lies merely 2.5 km away from the Mall. The famed Chambal gardens is situated at a distance of 5 km with Chambal river, the only perennial river in Rajasthan and the magnificent Kota Fort lying not very far from the Mall’s location. The catchments area of the City Mall will cover a target population of 2 million making it the most investor friendly location.
